# Video → ad pipeline (drop a video, get a proposed ad)

Turns raw footage into a PAUSED, ready-for-review video ad. Design decisions (2026-06-23):
copy + "vibe" are generated by the **agent** (not an API); the pipeline always produces a
**PAUSED** ad for human/agent approval (never auto-publishes live); built **foundation-first**.

## Flow

1. **Drop the video** in `data/video_intake/<account_slug>/inbox/` (or pass `--file`).
2. **`intake-video --account <slug>`** — locally extracts audio + sample frames (ffmpeg) and
   transcribes with faster-whisper, writing `creative_brief.json` (transcript, duration, frame
   paths, empty `copy_options`/`suggested_adset` for the agent to fill).
   - Requires the `media` extra (`pip install -e .[media]`) and `ffmpeg`/`ffprobe` on PATH.
3. **Agent step (in the loop):** read the brief (+ optionally the frames), the proven-winner
   swipe file **`knowledge/accounts/<account>/winning_copy.md`** (use it as the base — required),
   `knowledge/ad_copy_best_practices.md`, and the account profile/recent `metrics`, then draft
   **5 primary texts, 5 headlines, 5 descriptions** and pick the **best-fit ad set** (which is
   what provides the ROAS optimization — a new ad inherits its ad set's conversion/value
   optimization). Confirm the ad-set choice with the operator if unsure.
4. **`upload-video --account <slug> --file <path>`** — uploads to Meta, polls until `ready`,
   prints the `video_id`.
5. **`propose-video-ad --account <slug> --adset-id <id> --video-id <id> --page-id <id>
   --name … --message … --link … [--title … --description … --cta …]`** — writes a PAUSED
   `create_video_ad` authoring plan with the chosen copy.
6. **Approve + create:** set the op `status` to `approved`, then
   `apply-authoring --validate-only` (real Meta check) → `apply-authoring --execute` (creates
   the ad **PAUSED**).
7. **Go live:** when you're ready, enable it with `apply-ops set_status ACTIVE` (separately
   approved). Then measure with `metrics` after the learning phase.

## Notes / current limits
- **"Optimize for high ROAS" is the ad set's job**, not the ad's — the pipeline's leverage is
  putting the ad in the right, conversion/value-optimized ad set.
- A **thumbnail** may be required: upload one with the image-upload path and pass `--image-hash`
  if Meta rejects the ad without it.
- The Facebook **page_id** is required for the video creative (Divine Designs: `766046059925199`).
- **Not yet built:** building ads from *new* still images (vs video), multi-variant A/B creation
  of all 5 options at once, and auto-thumbnail selection. Next phases.
